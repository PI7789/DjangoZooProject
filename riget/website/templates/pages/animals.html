Our Animals

Welcome to the heart of our zoo, where you can discover a diverse array of animals from around the globe! Our mission is to educate and inspire visitors about wildlife conservation and the importance of protecting their natural habitats.
Mammals
African Elephants

Our majestic African elephants are the largest land animals on Earth. These gentle giants are known for their intelligence and social behavior. Visit them in their spacious habitat, where you can observe their interactions and learn about their role in the ecosystem.

Snow Leopards

These elusive big cats are native to the mountain ranges of Central and South Asia. With their beautiful spotted coats and incredible agility, snow leopards are perfectly adapted to their cold environments. Join us for a feeding session and watch them in action!

Birds
Bald Eagles

As a symbol of strength and freedom, our bald eagles are a must-see. You can watch them soar through the sky during our daily flight demonstrations and learn about their role in the ecosystem as apex predators.

Parrots

Colorful and intelligent, our parrots are a favorite among visitors. With their ability to mimic sounds and their playful personalities, they bring joy to all who visit. Don’t miss our interactive sessions where you can learn more about their behaviors.

Reptiles
Green Iguanas

These fascinating reptiles are known for their vibrant green color and impressive size. Our green iguanas can often be seen basking in the sun, showcasing their unique adaptations.

King Cobras

As the longest venomous snake in the world, king cobras are both awe-inspiring and a bit intimidating. Visit our reptile house to learn about their habitat, diet, and the vital role they play in controlling rodent populations.
Conservation Efforts
We are committed to the conservation of all species in our care. Our team actively participates in breeding programs, habitat restoration, and educational outreach. By visiting our zoo, you support these important efforts and help ensure a future for wildlife.

Plan Your Visit

Join us for an unforgettable experience! Check our schedule for special animal encounters, keeper talks, and feeding times. We can’t wait to share our passion for animals with you!
